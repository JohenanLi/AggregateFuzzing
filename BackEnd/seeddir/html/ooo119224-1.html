DOD builds a new malware-immune computer operating system, code-named Cobra. An epic struggle erupts between the Mossad and the KGB, pitting Colonel Oleg Najasvensky, KGB, against Mossad agent Brandon Nishri, and his exquisitely beautiful handler, Monica Freeman. Who steals Cobra, the Mossad, or the KGB?

Will the love between an Ames, Iowa farmer's daughter and a KGB assassin, survive a struggle between the Mossad and the KGB?
